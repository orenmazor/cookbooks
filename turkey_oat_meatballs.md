# Ingredients

- [ ] 1.5 lbs lean ground turkey
- [ ] 1 tsp sea salt
- [ ] 1 tsp dry or regular mustard
- [ ] 1 tsp smoked paprika
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] 3/4 cup oats
- [ ] 2 cloves chopped garlic
- [ ] lots of fresh parsley
- [ ] 2 tbsp tomato sauce

# Steps

1. preheat oven to 400f.
2. combine everything
3. make 1" meatballs.
4. bake 15-20 minutes.
